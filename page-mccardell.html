Grants
The McCardell Grant (or Board of Associates Grant) is a competitive grant program offered by Hood College to support faculty research and professional development. The Board of Associates is a group of alumni, parents, and friends of Hood College who provide funding for the grants. The Grants are awarded annually to faculty members who propose innovative research projects or initiatives that contribute to their field of study. The grants can be used for a variety of purposes, including research travel, equipment and supplies, and conference attendance.
