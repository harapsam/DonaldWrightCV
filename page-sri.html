The Summer Research Institute (SRI) at Hood College is a program that provides undergraduate students with the opportunity to engage in independent research projects under the guidance of Hood College faculty members. The program runs for 10 weeks during the summer and is open to students in all disciplines. During the SRI program, students work closely with a faculty mentor to develop a research project and carry out their investigations. At the end of the 10-week program, students present their research findings to the Hood College community and to other undergraduate researchers at regional conferences.
